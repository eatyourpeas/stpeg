Who We Are

The South Thames Paediatric Endocrine Group (STPEG) organises a biannual conference attended by approximately 100 healthcare professionals who work in paediatric endocrinology across hospitals in South East England.

Our network spans from King's and Guy's in London, extending out to Margate on the east coast and Brighton and Hastings on the south coast, bringing together clinicians, researchers, and healthcare professionals dedicated to advancing paediatric endocrinology care.

What We Represent

STPEG represents a collaborative network of professionals committed to:

  • Excellence in paediatric endocrine care
  • Professional development and education
  • Research and clinical innovation
  • Child and family-centered healthcare
  • Regional collaboration and knowledge sharing
